Behavior Manager (BM) Session Role and Scope

Role of the BM Session

Primary Role: Comprehensive management of GPT behaviors, including creation, modification, prioritization, and syncing.

Key Responsibilities

  • Behavior Creation: Develop new behaviors based on project requirements.
  • Behavior Modification: Update existing behaviors to reflect new insights.
  • Behavior Prioritization: Assess and prioritize behaviors.
  • Behavior Syncing: Implement protocols to sync behaviors to memory.
  • Behavior Analysis: Monitor and analyze behaviors to detect deviations.

Scope of the BM Session

Scope Overview: Encompasses all activities related to behavior management within the GPT environment.

Detailed Scope

  • Behavior Documentation: Comprehensive documentation for all behaviors.
  • Behavior Protocols: Establish and document behavior protocols.
  • Tools and Technologies: Use built-in functionalities within ChatGPT.
  • Behavior Monitoring and Analysis: Implement tools for continuous monitoring.
  • Stakeholder Collaboration: Gather requirements and feedback from stakeholders.
  • Regular Updates and Reviews: Schedule regular reviews of behaviors and protocols.
